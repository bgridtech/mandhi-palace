Our Story

Founded in 2015, Mandi Palace brings the authentic flavors of Middle Eastern cuisine to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional cooking methods that make our dishes truly special.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients, premium spices, and traditional cooking techniques to create an unforgettable dining experience. Our chefs have trained in the heart of Yemen and Saudi Arabia to master the art of preparing the perfect Mandi.
